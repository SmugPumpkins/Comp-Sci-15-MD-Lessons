# Mastery Rubric
## 5 – Mastery of Skill
* Demonstrates expertise and deep understanding of the content.
* Demonstrates strong passion and sustained effort.
* Is self-driven in learning and problem solving; independently finds solutions and resources.
## 4 – Excellence in Skill
* Demonstrates confidence and reliable understanding of the content.
* Demonstrates clear passion and consistent effort.
* Learns and solves problems successfully with some guidance.
## 3 – Proficiency in Skill
* Demonstrates a basic but accurate understanding of the content.
* Demonstrates some passion or effort.
* Asks for help when needed and uses feedback appropriately.
## 2 – Attempting Skill
* Demonstrates minimal or partial understanding of the content.
* Demonstrates inconsistent passion or effort.
* Sometimes seeks help but struggles to use guidance effectively.
## 1 – Starting to Attempt Skill
* Demonstrates very limited understanding of the content.
* Demonstrates little passion or effort.
* Rarely seeks help and shows difficulty engaging with guidance.
## 0 – Refusal to Engage with Skill
* Demonstrates no understanding of the content.
* Demonstrates no passion or effort.
* Makes no attempt to learn or participate.
